About TechStore

Your trusted partner in technology for over a decade, bringing you the latest innovations and exceptional service.

Our Story

Founded in 2013, TechStore began as a small family business with a simple mission: to make cutting-edge technology accessible to everyone. What started as a modest electronics store in downtown San Francisco has evolved into one of the most trusted online technology retailers in the United States.

Our founder, Michael Rodriguez, was a software engineer who noticed that many people struggled to find reliable, fairly-priced electronics. He saw an opportunity to bridge the gap between manufacturers and consumers by providing expert guidance, competitive pricing, and exceptional customer service.

Over the years, we've grown from a single storefront to a comprehensive online platform serving customers nationwide. Despite our growth, we've maintained our core values: integrity, innovation, and customer-first service. Every product we sell is carefully selected and tested by our team of technology experts.

Today, TechStore is proud to be a certified retailer for all major technology brands, offering everything from smartphones and laptops to smart home devices and gaming equipment. We've served over 500,000 satisfied customers and continue to expand our offerings based on the latest technological trends and customer feedback.
